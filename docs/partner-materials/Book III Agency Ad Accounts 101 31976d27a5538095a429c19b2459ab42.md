# Book III: Agency Ad Accounts 101

This book outlines how agency ad accounts work and how they differ from regular ad accounts in terms of stability, approvals, bans, and CPM performance. You’ll learn why infrastructure matters, what benefits agency accounts provide, how onboarding works, and how internal Meta ratings like HiVA and CAR impact your campaign results. It is a practical overview of what to expect when running ads with Uproas agency ad accounts.

### **1. Introduction**

Scaling on Meta becomes difficult when your ads run inside Business Managers that **lack trust.** Regular ad accounts are more likely to trigger reviews, get ads rejected, face delivery issues, or be disabled unexpectedly. These problems often come from the infrastructure behind the account rather than your strategy or creatives.

Agency ad accounts operate inside Business Managers that **already spend millions every month** and have long histories of clean, consistent advertising. Meta treats these environments as stable. This results in faster reviews, fewer disruptions, more predictable delivery, and the ability to scale without constant **bans & restrictions.**

This book explains what agency ad accounts are, how they work, and why strong infrastructure creates such a significant advantage for advertisers.

### **2. The Benefits of Using Agency Ad Accounts**

- ***Fewer Bans and Disabled Accounts***

Regular ad accounts often face restrictions from payment delays, getting banned due to ad rejections or getting hammered by meta’s automated system. Agency accounts avoid most of these issues because their Business Managers have **years of stable spend** and clean behavior. Meta already recognizes these agencies as reliable, which significantly **reduces bans or restrictions.**

- ***Ability to Spend Large Amounts From Day One***

Regular accounts usually require warm-up. Scaling too quickly triggers limits & reviews. Agency ad accounts behave differently. Because they operate inside Business Managers that consistently handle millions in monthly spend, advertisers can **start spending** how much **they want immediately** and scale quickly without triggering concerns.

- ***Faster and More Reliable Ad Approvals***

On regular accounts, approvals can take hours and sometimes produce inconsistent decisions. Agency accounts benefit from faster, more predictable reviews. **Most ads are approved within minutes,** and incorrect rejections occur far less often.

- ***Lower CPMs Under Platinum HiVA***

HiVA is Meta’s internal trust score for Business Managers based on long-term spend. While most providers sit at Gold HiVA, Uproas operates at **Platinum HiVA** with over **thirty million dollars in monthly spend.** Platinum HiVA receives preferential treatment in the auction system, resulting in stronger delivery and lower CPMs across campaigns.

- ***Run Ads For Almost Any Vertical***

Agency accounts can operate smoothly across many industries because Meta representatives can manually review creatives, give guidance on required adjustments, and help ensure compliance before ads go live. This eliminates guesswork and provides clarity in **borderline categories** that usually experience heavy review pressure on regular accounts.

- ***Q4 Stability***

Q4 is the most competitive period on Meta. Systems tighten, support becomes limited, and performance becomes parabolic. A disabled account during November or December can destroy an entire business.

Agency accounts reduce this risk through stronger trust signals and direct escalation routes. If an issue occurs, accounts can often be **restored quickly or replaced immediately,** keeping campaigns active throughout the most important weeks of the year.

- ***Avoiding Health Categorization***

Some Business Managers receive health categorization tag that restricts reach and raises CPMs. With Agency Ad Accounts you can **break free from health categorization** issues and bring your CPMs back to normal levels & scale up as much as you want.

### **3. How You Get Access to an Agency Ad Account**

- ***You Get Invited to the Agency’s Business Manager***

Uproas **sends you an invitation to your business manager.** After accepting the ad account to your business manager, the ad account appears instantly inside Ads Manager. You do not need a new profile or new Business Manager unless your current one has a problematic history.

- ***You Fund the Account Through Top-Ups***

Agency accounts use a **top-up system.** You add balance through Uproas, and your campaigns spend from that balance. This creates a stable billing environment and ensures any funding-related issues can be handled quickly.

- ***You Run Ads Exactly the Same Way as Before***

Your pixel, events, catalog, audiences, and reporting remain fully yours. The account behaves like any other ad account in Ads Manager. Uproas does not access your Ads Manager unless you specifically request assistance.

- ***If Something Happens, Support Is Immediate***

If an issue occurs, Uproas **escalates it directly to Meta.** Many accounts can be **restored,** and if not, a **replacement account is provided** so your campaigns continue running without downtime.

- ***You Can Request Additional Accounts Anytime***

If you need more ad accounts for testing, new markets, or new products, they can be added quickly. **There is no limit to how many accounts you can operate.**

- ***You Maintain Full Control of Campaigns***

You manage everything inside your Ads Manager. Uproas does not see your advertising activity **unless you explicitly request help.** Your privacy is fully respected.

- ***No Long-Term Lock-In***

There are **no long-term commitments.** If you stop using the accounts, access is removed and your assets remain intact.

### **4. The Downsides and Why They Are Still Worth It**

- ***Monthly Infrastructure Fee***

Agency ad accounts have a **monthly infrastructure fee.** For advertisers spending only twenty to fifty dollars per day, this cost usually isn’t worth it yet. But once you reach a few hundred dollars per day, the stability and performance improvements quickly outweigh the fee. For brands spending four-, five-, or six-figure budgets per day, the fee **becomes simple insurance,** protecting your revenue from bans and downtime.

- ***Top-Ups Require Cash Flow Planning***

Since agency accounts run on a top-up balance, you must keep your account funded. If the balance hits zero, your ads pause and learning phases reset. This is easy to manage by **checking your balance** regularly and keeping a **small buffer** during scaling.

- ***You Don’t Own the Ad Account***

The ad account **belongs to the agency’s Business Manager, not yours.** It cannot be moved or renamed. However, everything that matters remains fully in your control, including your pixel, events, audiences, catalogs, creatives, and all performance data.

- ***Not All Providers Are Equal***

The quality of agency ad account providers varies significantly. Many operate on weak HiVA levels, unstable Business Managers, or reseller setups with slow support. **Most providers sit at Gold HiVA,** which has weaker auction performance. **Uproas operates at Platinum HiVA** with more than $30M in monthly spend, backed by Double Green CAR, giving one of the strongest and most stable infrastructures available.

### **5. Understanding HiVA and CAR Ratings**

### **What HiVA Means**

HiVA is Meta’s internal rating that reflects long-term advertising value and spend consistency. Business Managers fall into three main tiers: **Silver, Gold, and Platinum.** Most providers operate at Gold. Uproas operates at Platinum, driven by more than thirty million dollars in monthly spend. Higher HiVA levels experience more reliable delivery and stronger auction performance.

### **What CAR Means**

**CAR** is Meta’s internal health rating that reflects how many operational flags a Business Manager has accumulated. These include excessive disapprovals, payment issues, or banned ad accounts. Meta representatives describe CAR using color states. **Uproas maintains Double Green CAR,** indicating clean and trustworthy operation.

### **How These Scores Affect You**

Platinum HiVA combined with Double Green CAR provides **one of the most stable advertising environments available on Meta.** This internal trust is a major reason agency ad accounts perform more consistently and scale more safely than regular accounts.
