## 

### **The Core Principle — Structure Protects Everything**

Most advertisers lose entire setups because everything sits inside one Business Manager.

When that account is flagged, the Pixel, Page, and Domain either get flagged or go down together.

The solution is simple but powerful: **separate and layer your assets.**

Each layer has a specific purpose—storage, execution, and support—so if one part is hit, the rest stay clean and reusable.

This approach mirrors how professional agencies operate and is the foundation of a truly bulletproof setup.

## 

### **Safe Business Manager (Pixel BM)**

This BM exists purely for **data and profile safety,** not ad execution.

It doesn’t hold Pages or Domains—only your **Pixel** and a **safe, aged profile** kept fully detached from risky activity.

### **Purpose:**

- Store and grow clean pixel data over time
- Keep the safe, aged profile detached from risky activity
- Maintain long-term trust and stability across future setups
- Share Pixel access to Execution BMs without exposing core assets

### **Contains:**

- Pixel(s)
- Safe aged profile(s)

You never run ads from this BM. Its job is to remain clean and untouched, ensuring your tracking data is always reusable no matter how many ad accounts or execution profiles come and go.

## 

### **Execution Business Manager (Media Buying BM)**

This is where all campaign activity happens.

It holds your **Pages, Ad Accounts, and Execution Profiles** that interact daily with Meta.

### **Purpose:**

- Launch, test, and scale campaigns
- Manage and connect Pages
- Receive shared Pixel data from your Safe BM
- Connect Agency Ad Accounts for execution

### **Contains:**

- Pages
- Ad Accounts ***(Uproas Agency Accounts recommended)***
- Execution Profiles (Admins + Staff)

If this BM ever gets flagged or restricted, your Pixel BM remains unaffected—allowing you to plug in new Business Managers and Ad Accounts and continue running your ads without losing Pixel data.

### **When something gets restricted:**

1. Pause all ads immediately.

2. Get a new **Execution Ad Account or Business Manager.** It’s always recommended to get a **new BM,** because once a BM carries flags, it becomes easier for future ad accounts under it to go down as well.

3. Request a new **Uproas Agency Ad Account.**

4. Reconnect your **Pixel** and **Profiles** from the Safe BM.

5. Relaunch your campaigns using the same data and creative sets.

Recovery is measured in hours because your core assets never go down.

### **Step-by-Step Facebook Setup Guide**

Now that you understand the system, here’s how to put it into practice.

Follow these steps to build your infrastructure from the ground up.

### **Step 1 — Create or Buy Facebook Assets**

You can either build assets yourself or purchase them ready-made.

Building them manually can take weeks of warm-up time, verification, and trust building.

For most advertisers, it’s far more efficient to **purchase verified Business Managers, Pages, and Accounts directly from [Uproas.io](https://www.uproas.io/)**, where each asset is pre-aged and ready for immediate use.

### **Step 2 — Download and Install Dolphin Anty**

Go to [**Dolphin Anty**](https://dolphin-anty.com/) and create your account.

Dolphin Anty is an **anti-detect (fingerprinting) browser** that lets you run multiple Facebook profiles safely without triggering cross-account detection.

- Download and install the software.
- Create your user account.
- You can start with up to 5–10 profiles for free before upgrading.

### **Step 3 — Buy ISP Proxies**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/67f7f9c71c5710baffe5a38f/692624ce98e00e40877d4785_Frame%202147235483.png)

‍

Next, visit [**Floxy.io**](https://www.floxy.io/) and purchase **ISP proxies.**

Select **USA** as your proxy location for the best internet speed.

These ISP proxies are highly stable and provide the most consistent results for Facebook advertising environments.

Make sure **each Facebook profile has its own unique proxy** to isolate sessions and prevent linkage.

Here’s a picture showing how to find your purchased proxy. Click on “Generate Proxy” from the dashboard -> Copy the proxy.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/67f7f9c71c5710baffe5a38f/6926253a84a8c07e6c6dd7bd_Frame%202147235484.png)

Inside Dolphin Anty:

2. Name the profile accordingly (Pixel BM, Media Buying BM, Pixel Profile (1,2), Media Buying Profile (1,2,3).

4. Choose **HTTP** as the connection type.Paste the exact value.

5. Paste the exact value.

Each profile becomes a clean environment for one Facebook asset—unique IP, fingerprint, and browser identity.

### **Step 5 — Log Into Your Facebook Assets**

Open the new browser profile you just created.

Log into one of your purchased Facebook accounts (each in its own profile).

Match **one account per proxy and per profile.**
