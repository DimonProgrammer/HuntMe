## How This Partnership Works

### Partnership Structure

- **You generate traffic.** You are responsible for sourcing candidate leads through job boards, social media ads, referrals, and other channels (detailed below).
- **HUNTME provides the tools.** HUNTME is a talent platform that provides call scripts, qualification criteria, and connects you with streaming agencies and studios looking for talent.
- **You recruit and qualify.** Your team uses our scripts to call candidates, qualify them against hard requirements, and book Zoom interviews.
- **Agencies conduct interviews.** HUNTME transfers qualified candidates to streaming agencies and studios (our clients). These agencies conduct interviews, select candidates, and handle onboarding.
- **You get paid per completed interview.** Payment is made for interviews where the candidate shows up and completes the interview process with the agency/studio.

### What You Need to Start

- **Telephony:** Your own phone system (VoIP, cloud dialer, or traditional). Call recording required for quality assurance.
- **CRM:** Your own candidate tracking system (Google Sheets, Airtable, or any CRM). We provide reporting templates.
- **Team:** Recruitment managers trained on our scripts (training time: 30 minutes using documents 01-02).

## Lead Generation Strategy

### Recommended Traffic Sources

We recommend a mix of **job boards** (primary), **social media ads** (secondary), and **referrals** (high conversion). Use generic job titles to maximize lead volume — qualify specifics during the call.

### Job Boards (Primary Source)

**Strategy:** Post generalized remote job ads. Avoid mentioning "HUNTME" or specific streaming platforms. Use broad titles like "Stream Manager," "Content Manager," "Chat Moderator."

**Europe:**

- Indeed Europe (indeed.co.uk, indeed.de, indeed.fr, indeed.es)
- StepStone (Germany, Belgium, Netherlands)
- Reed.co.uk, TotalJobs (UK)
- Jobrapido (pan-European)
- Local job boards (Work.ua for Ukraine, Rabota.ru for Russia, etc.)

**Latin America:**

- Bumeran (Argentina, Mexico, Chile, Peru)
- CompuTrabajo (Mexico, Colombia, Peru, Chile)
- Trabajando.com (regional)
- OLX (regional classifieds — Argentina, Colombia, etc.)

**USA / North America:**

- Indeed (primary)
- LinkedIn
- Glassdoor
- ZipRecruiter
- Monster

### Facebook / Instagram Ads (Secondary Source)

**Strategy:** Run lead generation ads with generic messaging. Target demographics: women 18-25 (Models), tech-savvy 18+ (Operators). Use phrases like "Remote Work Opportunity," "Become a Stream Manager," "Work From Home."

**Creative Examples:**

- "Earn $700+/month working remotely as a Content Manager. Flexible hours. Apply now."
- "Become a Stream Manager — no experience needed. Full training provided."
- "Remote Chat Moderator position. Work from home. $150+/week."

**Landing Page:** Simple form collecting Name, Age, Phone, Telegram/WhatsApp. Follow up with calls using our scripts.

### Referrals (High Conversion)

**Strategy:** Ask every candidate at the end of the call: "Do you know anyone who might be interested in a similar opportunity?" If yes, get their contact info and note the referrer's name.

**Referral Script:** "Hi [Name], [Referrer Name] recommended you for a remote position we're filling. Do you have 5 minutes to hear about it?"

Referrals convert 2-3x higher than cold traffic because of built-in social proof.

> **Important:** Never mention "HUNTME" in job ads or social media. Use the talent agency brand name assigned to your campaign (see Call Scripts for brand details).

## Performance Benchmarks & KPIs

### Manager Performance Targets (Experienced, 8-hour shift)

- **Calls per day:** 150
- **Interviews booked per day:** 16 (8 Models + 8 Operators, or 16 of one campaign)
- **Booking rate:** ~10-11% (16 bookings / 150 calls)
- **Show-up rate:** 40%+ (target: 60% with proper follow-up)
- **Completed interviews per day:** 6-7 (with 40% show-up rate)
- **Qualification accuracy:** 90%+ (don't book unqualified candidates)

### Traffic-Specific Conversion Rates

- **Warm Traffic (job ad responses):** 15-25% booking rate
- **Cold Traffic (outbound database calls):** 5-10% booking rate
- **Referral Traffic:** 25-40% booking rate

Mix traffic sources to optimize for volume and conversion. Warm + Referral = higher efficiency.

> **Show-Up Rate Optimization:** Send the interview confirmation message **within 5 minutes** of booking via Telegram or WhatsApp. This single action reduces no-shows by 30-40% and increases show-up rate from 40% to 60%+.

### New Manager Ramp-Up Timeline

- **Week 1:** 50-80 calls/day, 3-5 bookings/day (learning scripts, slow pace)
- **Week 2-3:** 100-120 calls/day, 8-10 bookings/day (building confidence)
- **Week 4+:** 150 calls/day, 15+ bookings/day (experienced performance)

Training time: 30 minutes (read Campaign Brief + Quick Start Guide). First 3 calls should be supervised.

- **You provide:** Your own phone system (VoIP, cloud dialer like Aircall/RingCentral, or traditional telephony).

### Reporting

- **Frequency:** Weekly reports submitted to HUNTME.
- **Required metrics:** Total calls, interviews booked, interviews completed (show-ups), booking rate, show-up rate.
- **Format:** CSV or Google Sheets (template provided upon partnership agreement).

## Legal & Compliance

### Data Privacy (GDPR Compliance)

- **Call recording consent:** Inform candidates at the beginning of the call that the conversation is being recorded for quality assurance and training purposes.
- **Data handling:** Candidate personal data (name, phone, age, location) must not be shared with third parties outside of HUNTME.
- **Data retention:** Retain candidate data only as long as necessary for recruitment purposes. Delete data upon request.

### Recruitment Compliance

- **Age verification:** Never recruit candidates under 18 for Models campaign. Operators can be 18+.
- **No misleading claims:** Do not promise guaranteed income, guaranteed placement, or make false claims about the role.
- **Respectful communication:** Follow the "Rule of One" — if a candidate says "not interested" twice, end the call politely.

> **Important:** Never improvise policy or make promises outside of the provided scripts. If a candidate asks about payment methods, tax documentation, legal contracts, or other edge cases, escalate to HUNTME support.
