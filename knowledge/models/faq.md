## What are the streams about?

You choose the theme yourself. Popular formats:

- Just chatting — conversations on any topic
- Beauty — makeup, skincare, fashion tips
- Unboxings and reviews
- Music, dance, creative content
- Entertainment and humor

The key is genuine, live interaction with viewers. A mentor helps you develop your content plan during training.

We stream simultaneously on **10+ platforms**, including:

- Bigo Live
- Uscreen
- StreamShark
- Dashark
- Trovo
- Wasd
- Dlive

The specific platform is assigned during training based on your profile and content direction.

## How does income work?

Two revenue streams — everything you earn is yours:

1. **Donations** — viewers send tips because they enjoy your personality, energy, or content
2. **Private chats** — one-on-one conversations charged per minute (this is the primary income source)

Starting income: from **$700/month** for beginners. Income grows proportionally with audience loyalty — most models reach **$2,000+/month** by month 3.

---

## Do I need to speak a foreign language?

No. The platforms have a **built-in translator**, so you can understand viewers immediately. Over time, if you want, you'll naturally pick up the language — but it's not a requirement to start.

---

## How long is the training?

The paid training period is **3-5 days (5 shifts)**. During training:

- You work alongside an experienced model who acts as your **curator**
- You receive **daily payouts** after each completed stream
- All software setup and platform onboarding is handled for you

After training, payouts switch to weekly (every Monday).

---

## What does the company get out of it?

We provide the platform access, technical support, audience promotion, and mentoring. In return, a percentage of the earnings is split between the company and the model. It's a mutually beneficial partnership — you earn, and we grow the platform and expand the audience.

---

## How long is the interview?

The interview is a **30-minute video call** — a private 1-on-1 conversation. You'll learn about the work process, ask questions, and decide afterward. **No obligation** — you're free to decline after the interview.

---

## Is this adult content / webcam?

No. This is **streaming entertainment** — similar to what influencers and content creators do on platforms like Instagram Live or Twitch. The content is entertainment-based: conversations, shows, music. Everything follows standard platform guidelines and you always control your own boundaries.

---

## Can anyone in my country see my streams?

No. All platforms use **geo-blocking** — viewers from your country cannot see your streams. Your identity is protected by geographic restrictions.
