## Brand & Representation Guidelines

When speaking with candidates, you represent a **talent agency** -- never mention "HUNTME" (this is an internal platform name only).

- Default brand: **[Company Name]** -- a talent agency specializing in developing and promoting streamers on international platforms.
- If your campaign is assigned a different client brand, use that brand name and URL instead.
- Use the company name consistently throughout the entire conversation. Substitute **[Company Name]** in all scripts below.
- If asked about the company website, provide: **[Company URL]**
- Positioning for the candidate: *"We are a talent agency that discovers, trains, and promotes streaming talent on international platforms."*

## Call Flow -- 5 Phases

| Phase | Name | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Opening | 0-15 sec |
| 2 | Quick Filter | 15-30 sec |
| 3 | Value Proposition | 2-3 min |
| 4 | Technical Qualification | 3-5 min |
| 5 | Closing | 1-2 min |

> **Key Principle:** Every phase must earn the candidate's permission to continue. Do not dump information -- engage, ask, listen. The goal is a **conversation**, not a monologue.

## Phase 1: Opening

**Goal:** The candidate stays on the line and agrees to listen -- 0-15 seconds

### Variant A -- Cold Call

**[COLD]**

> **SAY:** Hello, **[Name]**! My name is **[Your Name]**, I'm calling from **[Company Name]**. We're a talent agency that develops and promotes streamers. Right now we have an urgent opening for a **Remote Streaming Operator** -- $150+ per week, paid weekly, 100% remote. Can I take just 2 minutes to tell you the key points?

### Variant B -- Responded to Job Ad

**[WARM]**

> **SAY:** Hello, **[Name]**! My name is **[Your Name]** from **[Company Name]**. I see you responded to our vacancy for a **Streaming Platform Operator**. Great that you're interested! Let me walk you through the position details -- it'll take about 5 minutes. Shall we?

### Variant C -- Referral

**[REFERRAL]**

> **SAY:** Hello, **[Name]**! My name is **[Your Name]** from **[Company Name]**. **[Referrer Name]** recommended you as someone who might be a great fit for a remote position we have -- **Streaming Platform Operator**, from $150/week with growth. Do you have a couple of minutes?

### Handling Objections (All Variants)

**If "Not interested":** Use the rebuttal below. You get **one** soft attempt -- never push more than once.

> **REBUTTAL -- "Not Interested":** "I completely understand. Just so you know -- this is a fully remote position, weekly pay starts from $150, no experience needed. Could I just take 60 seconds to share the highlights? If it's not for you, no pressure at all."

**If "I'm busy right now":** Schedule a callback. Use the script below.

> **SAY:** No problem! When would be a convenient time to call back -- today in the evening or tomorrow morning?

**If "Who are you? / Where did you get my number?":** Use the rebuttal below to address the concern transparently.

> **REBUTTAL -- "Who Are You?":** "We're **[Company Name]**, a talent agency for streamers. Your profile came up as a potential match for our remote operator position. If this isn't relevant, I apologize for the interruption. But if remote work with weekly pay interests you, I'd love to share just the key points."

**STOP -- If candidate says "No" twice:** Thank the candidate politely and end the call. **Never push more than once.** A second "no" is final.

### Full Objection Handling Guide

Candidate raised an objection not covered above? See the Complete Objection Handling Guide (14 objections) -- includes "Is this a scam?", "$150 is too low", "I need to think about it", "I already have a job", and more. Each objection has a ready-to-use phone script and a 3-step framework (Acknowledge > Reframe > Bridge).

**If "Yes":** Proceed to Phase 2: Quick Filter.

## Phase 2: Quick Filter

**Goal:** Confirm PC and age -- 15-30 seconds

> **SAY:** Great! Just two quick questions before we dive in. First -- do you have a personal **PC or laptop**?

**STOP -- If no PC / laptop:** A personal computer is a **hard requirement** for this role. Use the soft handling below before ending the call.

> **REBUTTAL -- No PC:** "Are you planning to get a PC or laptop in the near future? If so, we can keep your profile and reach out when you're set up."

> **If no PC and no plans to get one:** Thank the candidate politely and end the call. Record the outcome in your CRM.

> **SAY:** Perfect. And may I ask -- how old are you?

**STOP -- If under 18:** Candidate is **not eligible**. "Unfortunately, this position is only available for candidates 18 and older." -- Thank the candidate politely and end the call.

> **SAY:** Excellent. Let me tell you more about the company, the role, and what you can earn.

**If both checks pass:** Proceed to Phase 3: Value Proposition.

## Phase 3: Value Proposition

**Goal:** Present the company, income trajectory, schedule, training, and social proof -- 2-3 minutes

### Company Overview

> **SAY:** The company specializes in developing and promoting streamers on online platforms. The operator's responsibilities are primarily related to the **technical and organizational side** of broadcasts.

### Key Responsibilities

- Equipment setup and control (PC and audio)
- Working with streaming software (OBS, Streamlabs, etc.) to improve image and sound quality
- Chat moderation, including: responding to messages, blocking rule violators, removing spam/ads/negative comments
- Supporting the streamer with content plan creation and adjustments
- Proposing creative ideas (encouraged by the company)

> **Reassurance:** **No prior experience required** -- full training is provided by the company. Emphasize this if the candidate sounds hesitant.

### Income -- Growth Trajectory

**Earning Potential:**

- **Starting base:** from **$150 per week**
- **With experience (1-2 months):** **$200-300 per week**
- **Top operators:** **$350-400+ per week**
- **That's $1,400-1,600/month** -- working from home, with weekly Sunday payments

Your income grows as your skills improve -- the company increases your **revenue share percentage** over time.

### Schedule

**Working Schedule:**

- **5/2 schedule**, shift-based: morning / afternoon / evening / night
- 6 to 8 hours per day
- 1 hour break, distributed freely within work hours

> **Important:** This is a **full-time position** with a 5/2 schedule (shift-based work). Make sure the candidate understands this before continuing.

### Training & Onboarding

**Onboarding Program:**

- **Duration:** 5-7 days
- Full shifts under supervision of an experienced operator
- **Paid training**
- Weekly payments, starting from **$30 per shift** on average

### Social Proof

**Our Team:** We currently work with operators across **more than 15 countries**. Many of our team members started with zero streaming experience and now earn **$300+/week** within their first 3 months.

## Phase 4: Technical Qualification

**Goal:** Confirm the candidate meets all technical and personal requirements -- 3-5 minutes

1. Are you currently studying or working?
   - *If studying: in-person or distance? (In-person students are NOT eligible)*
   - *If working: will you need more than 1 week notice period?*

**STOP -- If in-person student:** In-person students are **not eligible** for this full-time shift-based position. Explain politely and end the call.

2. Do you consider yourself a confident PC user?

3. Have you worked with OBS before?
   - *Record the answer; prior experience is not required.*

4. What is your English level?
   - *Minimum required: **B1 (Intermediate)***

**STOP -- If below B1:** Candidate is **not eligible** for English-speaking platforms. "Unfortunately, this position requires at least an Intermediate (B1) English level for communication on English-speaking streaming platforms." -- Thank the candidate politely and end the call.

5. Do you use a PC or laptop?

6. Does it have a LAN (Ethernet) port?

7. What is your processor model?
   - *Cross-reference with the compatible CPU list.*

8. What is your graphics card?
   - *Cross-reference with the compatible GPU list.*

> **If candidate doesn't know their specs:** Help them check:
> - **Windows:** Settings > System > About (shows processor)
> - **Quick method:** Press **Win+R**, type **"dxdiag"**, press Enter (shows both CPU and GPU)
> - **Task Manager:** Performance tab (shows CPU and GPU models)
> - **Alternative:** Ask them to send a screenshot via Telegram or WhatsApp if easier

9. What is your internet speed?
   - *Minimum required: 100 Mbps. If the candidate knows their speed, confirm it's 100+ Mbps. No need to run a speed test on the call.*

10. When would you be ready to start?
    - *Maximum acceptable delay: 1 week.*

11. Please share your Telegram username or WhatsApp number.
    - *Telegram format: @anna02 | WhatsApp format: +1 234 567-8900*

## Phase 5: Closing & Interview Booking

**Goal:** Schedule the interview with a confirmed date and time -- 1-2 minutes

> **SAY:** That covers all the key information about the position. Do you have any additional questions?

> **SAY:** If everything is clear, we'd like to invite you to an **online interview via Zoom**. Please make sure the app is installed in advance.

> **SAY:** What time would be most convenient for you to do the interview **tomorrow**?

> **Alternative Close Technique:** Do NOT ask an open-ended question. Instead, use an **alternative close** -- offer two specific time options: "Would **[Time A]** or **[Time B]** work better for you?"

> **After they pick a date/time:** Confirm the exact date, time, and format. Then deliver the passport/ID requirement:

> **SAY:** Perfect! I'll send you a confirmation message on Telegram / WhatsApp with all the details.
>
> One thing to mention -- please have your **passport or ID card** available during the interview. It's a standard requirement for age verification. The interviewer will just need to briefly see it on camera.
>
> We're looking forward to meeting you on **[date]**! If anything changes, just let us know in advance. Have a great day, **[Name]**!

> **If "I need to think about it" / hesitation:** See Objection Handling Guide > "I need to think about it" for detailed rebuttal scripts.

> **After confirmation:** Send the Interview Invitation Template via Telegram or WhatsApp within 5 minutes. This cements the commitment and reduces no-shows by 30-40%.

## Callback & Re-engagement Scripts

### Scheduled Callback

**When to use:** Use this script when the candidate asked you to call back at a specific time during the initial call.

> **SAY:** Hello **[Name]**, this is **[Your Name]** from **[Company Name]**. We spoke earlier about the operator position -- you asked me to call back at this time. Do you have a few minutes now?

### Re-engagement (Interested but Didn't Confirm)

**When to use:** Use this script when the candidate showed interest during a previous call but did not confirm an interview time.

> **SAY:** Hello **[Name]**, I'm following up on our conversation about the streaming operator position. Have you had a chance to think about it? We still have interview slots available this week.

## Interview Invitation -- Formal Version

### Scheduled Interview Details

Hello! Following up on our previous conversation, here are the details of your scheduled online interview for the **"Live Stream Operator"** position.

- **Date and time:** [Date], [Time]
- **Format:** Online, via Zoom

Please verify that your **microphone is working** on the device you will use to join the interview.

The Zoom link will be sent by the interviewer **5 minutes before the interview** via Telegram or WhatsApp. Please stay available and confirm that you have received the message.

Additionally, please have your **original passport or ID card** available during the interview for standard age verification purposes.

We look forward to seeing you at the scheduled time. If your plans change, please inform us in advance. Have a great day!
