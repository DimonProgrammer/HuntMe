Frequently Asked

Do you hire from the Philippines and Nigeria?

+

Yes — both countries are our primary markets. We have active operators from Metro Manila, Cebu, Davao, Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. Applications from these regions are processed fastest.

How do I receive payment in the Philippines or Nigeria?

+

Philippines: GCash or Wise (USD → PHP, same-day conversion). Nigeria: OPay, direct bank transfer (GTBank, Access, Zenith), or USDT. All payouts land every Sunday without exception.

Is this related to adult (18+) content?

+

No. We work exclusively with mainstream platforms similar to Twitch — gaming, music, talk shows, lifestyle. Zero adult content, full stop.

How do I know this isn't a scam?

+

We are a registered global talent agency. Zero upfront costs — ever. You receive payment starting from your very first training shift, every Sunday. If anyone asks you to pay anything, that is not us.

What happens during the interview?

+

A 30–40 minute video call where the studio explains everything: the role, platform, tools, schedule, and pay. You briefly show your ID for age verification. We do not collect copies or store your personal data.

Do I need streaming or OBS experience?

+

No prior experience required. Our 5–7 day paid training covers OBS Studio, moderation protocols, and content support step by step. You get a dedicated mentor throughout.

How exactly does the $30 training payout work?

+

Each training shift is approximately 6–8 hours. You receive ~$30 per shift. After your first completed week of training, you receive your first Sunday payout — just like all regular team members.
