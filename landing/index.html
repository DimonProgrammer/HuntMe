Frequently Asked

What countries do you hire from?

+

We hire globally — our team spans 15+ countries across Asia, Africa, Europe, and Latin America. The only requirements are a Windows PC meeting our specs, stable internet, and conversational English (B1+).

How do I receive payment?

+

Direct USD transfer every Monday. Payment method depends on your country and is discussed during the interview. We work with 15+ countries and find a convenient option for each.

Is this related to adult (18+) content?

+

No. We work exclusively with mainstream platforms similar to Twitch — gaming, music, talk shows, lifestyle. Zero adult content, full stop.

How do I know this isn't a scam?

+

We never charge you anything. Not before you start, not after. You receive payment starting from your very first training shift, every Monday. We never ask for bank access, passport scans, or "equipment deposits." If anyone asks you to pay, that is not us.

What happens during the interview?

+

A 30–40 minute video call where the studio explains everything: the role, platform, tools, schedule, and pay. You briefly show your ID for age verification. We do not collect copies or store your personal data.

Do I need streaming or OBS experience?

+

No prior experience required. Our 5–7 day paid training covers OBS Studio, moderation protocols, and content support step by step. You get a dedicated mentor throughout.

How exactly does the $30 training payout work?

+

Each training shift is approximately 6–8 hours. You receive ~$30 per shift. After your first completed week of training, you receive your first Monday payout — just like all regular team members.
