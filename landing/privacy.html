Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Who We Are

Apex Talent ("we", "us", "our") is a remote talent agency that recruits and trains streaming moderators and operators for mainstream live-streaming platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website at apextalent.pro.

2. Data We Collect

When you submit an application, we collect:

  • Full name
  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Country of residence
  • Age confirmation (18+)
  • English proficiency level
  • Current employment status

We may also collect basic technical data automatically (IP address, browser type, referring URL) via our hosting provider (Vercel) for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Evaluate your application for remote streaming positions
  • Contact you via WhatsApp with information about next steps
  • Send recruitment-related communications only

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Application data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and stored in a private Telegram channel accessible only to authorised Apex Talent staff. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access.

5. Cookies

Our website uses a single functional cookie to remember your cookie consent preference. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. You can dismiss the cookie banner to set this preference; it is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and not transmitted to any server.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@apextalent.pro or via WhatsApp at +7 919 642 7664.

7. Data Retention

If you are not onboarded as an operator, your application data is deleted within 90 days. Active operator records are retained for the duration of the working relationship and up to 12 months after it ends.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, email us at hello@apextalent.pro or reach us via WhatsApp: +7 919 642 7664
