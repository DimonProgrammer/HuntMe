What Does a Talent Scout Do?

Three simple steps — no management, just recruiting

Models stream — you earn

Models perform live streams — chatting, singing, dancing, playing games. They earn $1,500–$5,000/month from viewers' gifts and tips. Because models earn well, they want to work. And every shift they work — you automatically get $10. For 12 months.

Find, send, done

Find potential models on social media, job boards, or Telegram. Send them our ready-made scripts. Guide them to sign up. That's it — our team handles onboarding, training, and support after that.

Your income keeps growing

YOUR FIRST 7 DAYS

How to Get Your First Model
in 1 Week

Step-by-step. No guessing. We give you everything.

1
Day 1 — Get your toolkit

After joining, you get ready-made scripts, posting templates, and a personal team leader. Read through once — it takes 20 minutes. You'll know exactly what to say.

2
Days 2–3 — First outreach

Warm approach: send 15–20 DMs to girls you already know who might be interested. Cold approach: post in 3–5 Facebook/Telegram groups (remote work, income from home, freelance). Use the script exactly — don't change it. Both work. Pick what fits you.

3
Days 4–5 — Follow up & qualify

Most replies come on day 2–4. Use your objection-handling script. Only 2–3 questions determine if someone's a fit: age, available time, device. Your team leader helps with tricky cases.

4
Days 6–7 — Submit & earn

Pass the contact to your team leader. The studio handles the interview, training, and launch. Once the model works her first shift — your $10 starts coming in automatically. Every shift. For 12 months.
