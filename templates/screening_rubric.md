# Screening Rubric for Live Stream Moderator

## Hard Disqualifiers (auto-reject)

| Check | Condition | Result |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| PC/Laptop | No PC/laptop (Windows) | REJECT |
| Age | Under 18 or over 30 | REJECT |
| Study Status | In-person student | REJECT |
| CPU | Below 4 cores / 8 threads (i3 10th gen / Ryzen 3 3000) | REJECT |
| GPU | Integrated (no discrete GPU) | REJECT |
| MacBook | Apple device | REJECT |
| English | Below B1 (Intermediate) | REJECT |

## Scoring Criteria (each 1-10)

### 1. Hardware Compatibility (Weight: 30%)
- **8-10:** i5/i7 12th+ with RTX 3060+, 100+ Mbps, LAN port
- **5-7:** Meets minimum requirements (i3 10th+, discrete GPU GTX 1050+)
- **1-4:** Below minimum or could not verify

### 2. English Proficiency (Weight: 25%)
- **8-10:** B2-C1+, fluent, natural phrasing, no errors
- **5-7:** B1, some errors but clearly understandable
- **1-4:** Below B1, hard to understand (auto-reject)

### 3. Availability / Start Date (Weight: 20%)
- **8-10:** Ready within 1 week, flexible schedule, 6+ hrs/day
- **5-7:** Ready within 2 weeks, fixed but reasonable schedule
- **1-4:** Cannot start within 2 weeks, very limited hours

### 4. Motivation / Engagement (Weight: 15%)
- **8-10:** Enthusiastic, asks questions, detailed responses, professional
- **5-7:** Generic but positive responses, shows some interest
- **1-4:** Copy-pasted text, minimal effort, unresponsive

### 5. Experience (Weight: 10%)
- **8-10:** 2+ years VA/admin/support/customer service
- **5-7:** 1 year or adjacent online work experience
- **1-4:** No relevant experience (NOT a disqualifier)

## Decision Thresholds

| Score Range | Decision | Action |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| 70-100 | **PASS** | Send Zoom interview invitation, notify admin |
| 50-69 | **MAYBE** | Ask 1-2 clarifying questions, review manually |
| 0-49 | **REJECT** | Send polite decline with encouragement |

## Red Flags (auto-reject considerations)
- Cannot write a coherent sentence in English
- Claims MacBook or Apple device
- Availability under 4 hours/day
- Asks for advance payment
- Suspicious/bot-like responses
- Under 18 years old
- In-person student (cannot maintain 5/2 schedule)
