# Screening Rubric for Remote Chat Moderator

## Scoring Criteria (each 1-10)

### 1. English Proficiency (Weight: 30%)
- **8-10:** Fluent, natural phrasing, no errors, varied vocabulary
- **5-7:** Some errors but clearly understandable, basic vocabulary
- **1-4:** Major grammar issues, hard to understand, very limited

### 2. Availability (Weight: 25%)
- **8-10:** 6+ hours/day, flexible timezone, full-time preferred
- **5-7:** 4-5 hours/day, fixed but reasonable schedule
- **1-4:** Under 4 hours/day, very limited availability

### 3. Motivation (Weight: 20%)
- **8-10:** Specific reasons, enthusiastic, asks smart questions, professional
- **5-7:** Generic but positive response, shows some interest
- **1-4:** Copy-pasted text, minimal effort, no personalization

### 4. Equipment (Weight: 15%)
- **8-10:** PC/laptop + stable high-speed internet
- **5-7:** Phone + decent internet, or old PC
- **1-4:** No equipment mentioned, unreliable internet

### 5. Experience (Weight: 10%)
- **8-10:** 2+ years VA/admin/support/customer service
- **5-7:** 1 year or adjacent online work experience
- **1-4:** No relevant experience at all

## Decision Thresholds

| Score Range | Decision | Action |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| 70-100 | **PASS** | Send referral link immediately, notify admin |
| 50-69 | **MAYBE** | Ask clarifying questions, review manually |
| 0-49 | **REJECT** | Send polite decline with encouragement |

## Red Flags (auto-reject considerations)
- Cannot write a coherent sentence in English
- Availability under 2 hours/day
- Asks for advance payment
- Suspicious/bot-like responses
- Under 18 years old
